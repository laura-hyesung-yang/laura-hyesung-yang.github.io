Laura Hyesung Yang

I am a rising 2nd year Ph.D. student at Daniel Jacob's lab studying atmospheric chemistry modeling. I have a diverse environmental-related research experience in computational biology, environmental microbiome genomics, and empirical atmospheric chemistry. I am a Korean American (my middle name is my Korean name) and in my free time, I write poetry, doodle, and bike along the Charles river.
