Laura Hyesung Yang

I am a Ph.D. student at Daniel Jacob's lab specializing in modeling atmospheric chemistry. My research projects involve using a model, satellite data, and aircraft observations to better understand tropospheric oxidant and organic chemistry. I also explore the effect of the hydrogen economy on atmospheric chemistry and climate. I will be interning at NASA JPL from February to June 2025.
